> Notes for using OpenAPI with .NET, which makes it easy to call REST APIs

- `Microsoft.AspNetCore.OpenAPI`, available in .NET 9
- [Scalar](https://github.com/scalar/scalar/blob/main/integrations/aspnetcore/README.md)
